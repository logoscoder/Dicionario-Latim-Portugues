Master The reply is that although the
emperor can grant many freedoms to the
king of France and to other [kings], yet he
can not in any way totally separate the
kingdom of France or another [kingdom]
from the empire so that it is not in any way
under the empire, because this would be to
destroy the empire, something the emperor
can not do.
