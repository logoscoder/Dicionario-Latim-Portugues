CHAPTER 2

Student: I was intending to ask only about
men called spiritual in the second sense. Let
us go on with them, therefore, beginning
with the highest of them, that is the
supreme pontiff, investigating first whether
the emperor has or can have any power or
right in the election of the supreme pontiff
and second whether he has or can have any
power over him who is appointed supreme
pontiff. First, however, I want to investigate
whether the right of electing the supreme
pontiff can belong to the person of the
emperor.

DOES (OR CAN) THE EMPEROR HAVE ANY POWER IN RELATION TO THE POPE?
