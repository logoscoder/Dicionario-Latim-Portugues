Master The reply is that if cardinals become
heretics, however secretly, they do not
recover the right to elect the supreme pontiff,
even if they return to the orthodox faith and
wash away the defect of heretical wickedness
by penance, unless the same right is
conferred on them anew; and the same thing
is said about any cardinal at all if any of them
becomes a heretic while the others remain in
catholic truth because from that moment he
will not be able to elect unless the right to
elect is conferred on him anew.
