Master Many regard it as a heresy to say
that the pope can do anything without any
exception, even speaking about those
things which can be done by man. This is
(i) because he can do nothing which is
against divine law or natural law and (ii)
because there are many things which are
not against divine law or natural law that
he can not do, many of which indeed can
be done by others.
